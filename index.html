About Us

Holistic Healing in the Heart of Madurai

Welcome to Care n Cure Acupuncture Clinic, Madurai’s trusted destination for holistic healing and wellness—just steps from the heart of the city.

We specialize in authentic acupuncture treatments rooted in traditional wisdom and backed by modern understanding. Our clinic offers personalized care for a wide range of health concerns including chronic conditions, women's health, pain management, mental wellness, skin & beauty, and more.

We also offer Hijama (wet cupping therapy) and traditional cupping treatments, known for their detoxifying and rejuvenating effects.

As a certified Wellness Coach, our founder brings a compassionate, results-driven approach to every treatment. We believe in empowering our clients with knowledge, personalized care, and natural solutions that restore balance and vitality.
